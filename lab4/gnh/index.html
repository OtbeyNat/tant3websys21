Gamma Nu Eta is the National Information Technology Honor Society. This organization was founded to recognize and promote excellence in the study and the leadership of Information Technology, to offer knowledge and services to members' schools and the surrounding communities, and to play an active role in the application and evolution of Information Technology within society.
Furthermore, the organization is intended to foster scholarship, leadership, and community involvement among those members. In essence, we provide an environment that allows for Information Technology and Web Science students to thrive. As members must be strong academically, demonstrate leadership, show campus involvement, and display excellent character, we provide a strong foundation for those following us through services such as tutoring and other events.
