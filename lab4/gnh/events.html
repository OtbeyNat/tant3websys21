Events

Gamma Nu Eta is known for providing three main events and services: Company Expose, Tutoring, and Alumni Newsletter. Together, these function to provide a well-rounded organization that compels its members to succeed in their academics and future altogether.

Company Expose

Annually, GNH hosts the Information Techonology-exclusive Company Expose. It is the department's largest recruitment event in which students have the opportunity to network and interact with company representatives and recruiters in a casual setting. The parties introduce themselves to one another and socialize during games and other activities.

Join our Newsletter!

Newsletter

Every year, Gamma Nu Eta releases a newletter to ITWS and GNH alumni updating them about the ITWS department and the Institute itself.

Gamma Nu Eta Tutoring

Tutoring is conducted in-person at the ITWS Lounge, which is located on the second floor of the Lally Building, right across from Linda Kramarchyk's office. All you need to do is show up at the appropriate time to receive tutoring.

At these tutoring sessions, Gamma Nu Eta can help you with any course in your ITWS degree program. This includes ITWS core classes, or other essential courses such as Computer Science 1 or Data Structures . We can also help with any questions that you might have about career development, resumes, as well as just general questions about RPI.
